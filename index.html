Chest and Triceps (Day 1)

Chest

  • Normal pushups (3 sets of 20)

  • Decline pushups (3 sets of 10)

  • Incline Pushups (3 sets of 10)

  • Diamond pushups (3 sets of 8)

    • If you can’t do 8, do as many as you can for the set

Triceps

  • Bench dips (3 sets of 10)
  • Pike pushup (3 sets of 7)
  • Dive Bomber Pushups (3 sets of 5)

Biceps and Back (Day 2)

Biceps

  • Bicep Pushup (3 sets of 5)
  • Bicep Curls (3 sets of 10) [Can vary depending on what you use for weights]
  • Can do more of these exercises if you feel these are not enough.

Back

  • Supermans (3 sets of 10)
  • Aquamans (3 sets of 10)
  • Reverse Snow Angels (3 sets of 10)
  • Hip Hinges (3 sets of 10)

Shoulders (Day 3)

  • Crab Walk (3 sets of 10)
  • Push-back Push Up (3 sets of 10)
  • Pike Push-Up (3 sets of 10)
  • Yoga walk up (3 sets of 10)

Legs ( Day 4)

  • Squat ( 3 sets of 20 ) [Each leg]
  • Bulgarian split Squat ( 3 sets of 10 ) [Each leg]
  • Lunges ( 3 sets of 10 ) [Each leg]
  • Single Leg Hip Raise ( 3 sets of 10 ) [Each leg]
  • Hip Thrusts ( 3 sets of 10 )

Rest (Day 5)
